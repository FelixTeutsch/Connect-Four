Connect Four Rules:

  • The game is played on a vertical grid of 6 rows and 7 columns.
  • Players take turns dropping their colored discs from the top into any of the columns.
  • The goal is to connect four of your colored discs vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
  • If all the cells in a particular column are filled and a player still wants to make a move, they must choose a different column.
  • The Border of the field indicates whose players turn it is
